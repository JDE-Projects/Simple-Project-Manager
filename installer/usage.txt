Getting started: Launch Simple Project Manager, then click + Phase to begin
building your project as a nested checklist - add Steps inside a phase, and
Actions and Contacts inside a step. Click any item to set its owner, deadline,
status and priority; tick actions as you finish them and progress rolls up
automatically. Use Save to store the project as an ordinary Excel .xlsx file you
can reopen here or in Excel, and Open to load one back.
